Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip through Wanderlust?

You can book a trip by searching for your desired destination on our website, selecting accommodations and activities, and completing the booking process. Our customer service team is available to assist you at any step.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For certain destinations, we also offer payment plans to help you budget for your dream vacation.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, most reservations can be modified or canceled. Please check the specific terms and conditions for your booking, as cancellation policies vary by destination and accommodation type.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance packages that cover trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more. We recommend adding insurance to all bookings for peace of mind.
